Meet the President of RubberDucky!

The Ugly Duckling

In her early years, Katie DeMet was a shy child. Her mother used to take her after school to the local park almost every day where she worked as a vendor. Whenever Katie felt out of place with the other children she would wander off to the pond where she found the local flock of ducks to play with. Katie found feeding and watching the ducks to be so much fun, that she found herself looking forward to it.

The Hatching of RubberDucky

As Katie grew, her mother eventually found work closer to the city and the long evenings at the park ended. In tribute to her old duck friends, Katie began making duck replicas out of paper mache or popsickle sticks. Over the years she found herself collecting all kinds of duck merchandise and collecting pictures. When Katie finally got into coding in college, the plethora of duck figurines hit the internet and became a sensation on social media. Seeing the high demand for duck and duck figurine pictures lead Katie to create the RubberDucky site that we all know and love today!
